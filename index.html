Swedish National Heritage Board

Securing Legacy Systems, Delivered On Time

Facing a tight two-month deadline in early 2025, I stepped in as a DevSecOps Engineer and Solution Architect to secure the Swedish National Heritage Board’s aging systems while supporting their shift to new services. Poor planning had left their migration at risk—vague security goals and siloed teams threatened delays. I wrote detailed documentation and code aligning their Kubernetes setup with Zero Trust principles, integrating Spring Security and OAuth 2.0 to lock down access. By automating vulnerability scans with security tools, I caught risks early, cutting manual review time by weeks. The result? A secure, scalable system delivered on schedule with clear documentation and code, saving them from rework costs and keeping their timeline intact. Past work; no current affiliation.

Worldline

Fraud-Proof Transactions, Ahead of Schedule

From 2021 to 2024, I led Worldline’s DevSecOps efforts to modernize transaction systems and fight AML risks. Their old setup suffered from manual processes and fuzzy requirements, slowing merchant onboarding and risking fraud. I built serverless AWS Lambda solutions and microservices with Spring Boot 3, Python by automating risk analysis to slash review times from weeks to days. Clear goals kept us focused, while Jenkins pipelines cut deployment errors to a minimum. We delivered ahead of schedule, saving costs on manual fixes and boosting merchant trust with faster, safer onboarding. Past work; no current affiliation.

Vattenfall AB

Modernizing Energy Systems, On Time

From 2019 to 2021, I tackled Vattenfall’s outdated Energy Site system as a DevOps Software Engineer. Unrealistic deadlines and ignored technical debt had bogged down their electricity trading platform. I replaced Java EE 5 with Spring Boot 2 microservices, using Docker and Kubernetes to containerize and scale. Automated testing with JUnit 5 caught issues early, avoiding last-minute chaos, while Kafka synced old and new systems without a hitch. My detailed roadmap—built with the team—ensured we hit every deadline, cutting operational costs and boosting system reliability for their distributors. Past work; no current affiliation.

Inera AB - 1177 Vårdguiden

Streamlining Healthcare Admin, On Schedule

In 2018-2019, I overhauled Inera’s old admin system for 1177 Vårdguiden as a TDD System Developer. With siloed teams and unclear accountability had slowed progress. I built a Java EE 7 backend with Angular, automating data migration from old system to the new system with JPA to move clinic info seamlessly. Detailed automated system tests and improved automated pipelines and mob programming skyrocketed the productivity with less mistakes, while my collaboration with the solution architect clarified goals—like “admins manage 50 clinics daily with better and modern system.” We delivered on time, streamlining operations nationwide and saving rework costs. Past work; no current affiliation.

Swedish Police Force

Justice System Overhaul, On Time

From 2015 to 2017, I transformed the Swedish Police’s Judicial Information System from a creaky mainframe to a modern Java EE 6 and Spring Framework setup with an Angular frontend. Starting as a junior developer, I tackled poor planning and manual testing head-on, automating workflows with Jenkins and JUnit to save weeks of effort. I took over the BR-adapter mid-project, learning Spring fast to keep it on track. Clear goals—like “do what other teams couldn't accomplish”—and team collaboration delivered a scalable system on schedule, cutting huge maintenance costs. Past work; no current affiliation.
